Our Story

We Don't Just
Photograph — We Feel

Skyframe was born from a deep belief that a wedding is more than an event — it is the beginning of a lifelong story. Our team blends cinematic videography, precision drone aerials, and intimate photography to preserve every whispered vow, every joyful tear.

Based in Sri Lanka, we travel island-wide and internationally, bringing the same uncompromising eye for light and emotion to every venue we photograph.
